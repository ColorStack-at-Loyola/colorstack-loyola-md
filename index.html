Our Mission

ColorStack's mission is to increase the number of Black, Latinx, and Indigenous
technologists who graduate and go on to start rewarding technical careers.

Community

Build connections with peers and professionals in tech who share similar backgrounds and experiences.

Education

Access resources, workshops, and mentorship to help you succeed in your technical education and career.

Opportunity

Discover internships, scholarships, and job opportunities specifically for underrepresented students in tech.

What We Do

ColorStack LUM drives progress in tech by creating a welcoming space for students of all backgrounds to explore, grow, and thrive. Rooted in community and support, our chapter empowers members with the tools, guidance, and experiences needed to confidently pursue careers in technology and beyond.

We guide students toward success by providing practical tools, mentorship, and experiences that prepare them for life beyond the classroom. Whether it’s skill-building sessions, networking opportunities, or collaborative events, we’re helping students take confident steps toward their future careers.
