About the club

The ColorStack chapter at Loyola University Maryland was founded on October 2024. We are a community of students dedicated to empowering underrepresented students in tech through mentorship, networking, and professional development opportunities. Our club is a hub and resource to those who are pursuing tech majors like Computer Science, Data Science, Computer Engineering, etc. Our mission is to create a supportive environment where all students can thrive and succeed in their careers.

Through our programs, we support member success by connecting students with internships, fellowships, and major conferences like NSBE, AfroTech, Tapia, and Grace Hopper.
